Listing Description

Magnificent detached cottage with ample parking. It is located in Notre-Dame-de-Grâce on a quiet street. The backyard has a shed, 2 fruit trees, garden boxes and everything is ideal for barbecues with friends and family this summer. Inside, you will find 2 bedrooms upstairs with a powder room, the ground floor has a bathroom, kitchen, living room and dining room as well as a family room. The basement offers a family room, a small bedroom and a laundry room. Look at the photos, they speak for themselves. See Addendum for the program Integri-t. (54208109)
