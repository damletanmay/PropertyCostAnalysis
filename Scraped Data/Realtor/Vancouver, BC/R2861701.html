Listing Description

Discover luxury living in Vancouver West's prestigious Dunbar neighborhood with this exquisite custom-built home. Nestled on a tranquil 50x130 lot, this masterpiece boasts over 4531 square ft of opulent living space. Featuring 5 grand bedrooms, 6 bathrooms, and a plethora of high-end amenities including radiant heating, central air conditioning, a gourmet kitchen plus a wok kitchen, and a 3-car garage. Indulge in the lavish lifestyle with a wine room, media room, entertainment bar, and sauna. Situated in a top school catchment area, close to elite schools like Crofton House,St. George, Southlands Elementary, Point Grey Secondary and UBC, this home is a testament to luxury and elegance. Don't miss the opportunity to own a piece of Dunbar's finest. (29510819)
