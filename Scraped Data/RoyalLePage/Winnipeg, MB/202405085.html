Charming brick character home with unmatched curb appeal and a location that offers close proximity to numerous amenities including entry to the Assiniboine Park. This home features numerous original features but integrates modern amenities throughout. Main floor bedroom includes 4 pce bath with additional flex space currently used as a pantry and tv lounge but these extra spaces could be converted to a home gym or home office. Tons of natural light pours into the oversized living space, a large dining area and super functional kitchen with granite counters. The upper level offers two additional bedrooms, including primary with ensuite. Seldom offered in this vintage is an attached garage, fully fenced yard with deck and landscaped. Basement is undeveloped allowing the next owner to develop to suit. Upgrades incl. windows, shingles, bathrooms, flooring, paint and more! (id:7525)
